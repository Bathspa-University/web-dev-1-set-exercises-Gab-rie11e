Fried Rice

February 29, 2024

Fried rice is a popular dish and a staple in a few countries around the region of Asia. Savory, tasty, quick and easy to prepare as the dish does not require a lot of ingredients to make, in fact you can make a very simple fried rice with just left overd ingredients. The versatility of the the dish makes it easy to customize it to your liking.

Ingredients:

  • 5 cloves of Minced Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Leftover Rice
  • Salt to taste
  • Scallions
  • Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Oyster sauce

Method:

  1. First get a wok or a pan, turn up the heat to medium. Put some oil on your pan and wait until it's hot enough and ready to cook.
  2. Once the oil is hot enough cook a scrambled egg and once done set aside.
  3. Once again put oil, when ready add your garlic and onions and cook until the onion is tender and the garlic is brown.
  4. Add in your leftover rice, mix and saut√© to prevent the rice from clumping and get a well mix of the rice and the ingredients. Do this for 3-4 minutes.
  5. After cooking the rice for a few minutes add your soy sauce, oyster sauce, and egg. Mix them well together for 2-3 minutes until the rice has been coated with both sauces.
  6. Lastly, add salt to taste. Add however fits your taste.
  7. Finally, serve in a plate with some chopped scallions on top.
