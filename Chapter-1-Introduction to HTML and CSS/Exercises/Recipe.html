Fried Rice

February 29, 2024

Fried rice is a popular dish and a staple in a few countries around the region of Asia.
Savory, tasty, quick and easy to prepare as the dish does not require a lot of ingredients to make,
in fact you can make a very simple fried rice with just left overd ingredients.
The versatility of the the dish makes it easy to customize the dish to your liking.
