Overview:

• Final Fantasy VII •

The original Final Fantasy VII was first released in 1997. The game was directed by Yoshinori Kitase, Hironobu Sakaguchi as the executive producer and Shinji Hashimoto as the co-producer. The game was once being developed for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System in 1994, but as the project developed, it was moved to the Sony PlayStation because of the Nintendo's cartridge format's limitations. Higher-quality graphics and more expansive storytelling were made possible by this change. The game follows the traditional role-playing gameplay with turn-based combat system, exploration, power leveling, and individual unique skills that the characters can use.

• Final Fantasy VII Remake •

In 2015 Square Enix announced the remake of Final Fantasy VII. It was then released in April 10 of 2020. Fans all over the globe were very much excited for the much anticipated remake of the original Final Fantasy VII. The remake presents a numerous amounts of new features starting with graphics and combat system. The remake makes full use of the new PS5 console to put visuals and graphics to its full potential, the combat has been reworked into real time combat instead of the original turn-based system. Final Fantasy VII Remake boasts more brand new content like extended story, mini-games, map and environment, music, side quests, and many more.

Plot:

The protagonist of Final Fantasy VII, Cloud Strife, was formerly a member of the elite military group known as SOLDIER. In order to battle the dishonest megacorporation Shinra Electric Power Company, Cloud Strife joins the eco-terrorist group AVALANCHE.

AVALANCHE hired mercenary Cloud Strife to help the gang destroy a Mako Reactor, a facility that absorbs the planet's life force, known as Mako, for electricity, near the start of the game. Cloud meets other important people during the mission, such as childhood buddy Tifa Lockhart and AVALANCHE leader Barret Wallace. As the narrative develops, it becomes clear that Shinra is using military force to maintain control while profiting from the planet's resources. Shinra is also searching for a mysterious being called "Jenova" and hoping to use its strength for their own evil plans. Cloud's past comes back to haunt him as he learns about Sephiroth, a legendary SOLDIER believed to be dead, who resurfaces with plans to summon Meteor and bring about the planet's destruction. Sephiroth's connection to Cloud and the truth behind his past form significant elements of the plot's development. In an effort to stop Shinra and Sephiroth, Cloud sets out across the planet Gaia with his comrades. They go through a variety of places, such as the peaceful hamlet of Nibelheim, the bustling metropolis of Midgar, and the immense wilderness of the planet's surface.

Throughout the story, Cloud struggles to distinguish between his own memories and those that Shinra implanted during his experiments, leading to identity issues. His intentions are shaped and his character development is given depth by this internal conflict. The plot revolves around the idea of the Lifestream, which is the planet's life force. It is revealed that Sephiroth intends to use the Lifestream's power to grow into an entity with godlike qualities. This sets up Cloud and Sephiroth for a dramatic clash atop the Northern Crater. In order to stop Meteor from crashing into the Earth, Cloud and his allies face Sephiroth and his weapon, Jenova Synthesis, in the story's finale. By means of perseverance, selflessness, and the strength of camaraderie, they manage to prevent the annihilation of Gaia. The battle's aftermath, when Cloud and his companions process what happened and how it changed their lives, forms the story's climax. The story ends with hope for the future as they work to restore and cure the earth.

Characters:

• Cloud Strife •

Cloud Strife, the protagonist of Final Fantasy VII. A complex individual with a somber past and calm demeanor. The path of Cloud reveals layers of internal conflict and weakness. He battles identity and purpose issues and is plagued by recollections of his time spent in SOLDIER.

• Aerith Gainsborough •

Aerith is a flower vendor with enigmatic abilities, and Shinra is after her because of her ties to the extinct Cetra race. She is a hopeful and compassionate person who acts as Cloud's love interest.

• Tifa Lockhart •

Tifa, an AVALANCHE member and Cloud's childhood friend, is the manager of Seventh Heaven, a bar located in Sector 7 of Midgar. A strong and skilled martial artist.

• Barret Wallace •

The leader of AVALANCHE's Midgar cell Barret. Motivated by a strong sense of justice against Shinra's plunder of the planet's resources, he aims to stop shinra from the injustice and corruption the organization has caused.

• Vincent Valentine •

The character of Vincent Valentine is enigmatic and elusive. Formerly a part of the exclusive Shinra group known as the Turks. Vincent tends to hide his inner anguish and haunting memories behind an aloof and disconnected attitude. Vincent is a strong fighter with a strong desire to support his allies in their struggle against Shinra and the evil Sephiroth, despite his difficult past.

• Red XIII •

Red XIII, also known as Nanaki, is a tattooed, intelligent, and noble beast with fiery red fur. Red XIII exhibits extraordinary knowledge and awareness despite his animalistic appearance, frequently offering insightful viewpoints on the group's voyage. He is a custodian of the planet's historical information and has a close connection to it.

• Sephiroth •

Sephiroth, the main antagonist of Final Fantasy VII. A formidable and evil character. Once regarded as a legendary hero in SOLDIER, Sephiroth's journey toward darkness is characterized by his realization of his true identity and a twisted understanding of his mission. Sephiroth is intelligent and has immense physical prowess, who is always steps ahead of everyone and excels among all the other members of SOLDIER and the rest of characters.
